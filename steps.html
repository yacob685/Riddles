Installation Guide

Get the App on your device

iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1

Open the translator in Safari.

2

Tap the Share button in the toolbar.

3

Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen.

Android

1

Open the translator in Chrome.

2

Tap the three dots in the top right corner.

3

Tap Install App or Add to Home screen.

Windows / Mac (Edge & Chrome)

1

Look for the Install icon in the right side of the address bar.

2

Click it and select Install to launch as a standalone app.
